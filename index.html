Past Development Projects

Some projects that I worked on in teams and personal projects

Streamful

Streamful is a site that I designed and built with a team. It allows users to access live streams from all the top live streaming services in one convenient place. The project was centered around incorporating APIs into a hosted site. A secondary objective was to include accessibility functions. We built this using the Django web framework.

Fabrication Projects

As a professional fabricatorI worked at every level of the custom fabrication process. The shop was small enough that I did client intake, project design, project management, CAD/CAM, fabrication or installation and any combination of the above. Here are just a few projects that I worked on.

I worked on two scupltures that were for Ramen restaurants in Texas. This one resides in Houston and a black on is in Austin. The cat was sculpted, finished and shot with hardcoat before paint.

Johnson and Johnson Meeting Room

A fiberglass room was made for the entry of the Johnson and Johnson lab in Houston. We fabbed the panels and covered them in fiberglass. We arranged the panels in a mock-up room in our shop in Austin to ensure soomth fit. We then shot the panels in the shop individually before driving the panels out to Houston. There I lead a team to set the panels up, seam the panels with fiberglass on site, and finally shoot the seams and buff it out to a perfectly smooth finish.

Shiner Ram Skull

A ram skull complete with red eyes and a fog machine set to intermittent emmision. This piece shows a bit of the different things we do with the sculptures. Electrical, kinetic and functional elements were a big part of making clients visions become reality.
